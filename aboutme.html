About Me

Hello, I'm Ruo Yu Wu (Chloe), a UI/UX and Product Designer based in London.

With a background in Industrial Design, I specialize in User Research, UI/UX Design, and Product Strategy to craft intuitive and impactful digital experiences. I thrive on solving user pain points through research-driven design, ensuring every solution is both functional and meaningful.

I have experience designing Cross-Platform Interfaces for 3D printing applications, working on both web and mobile platforms to enhance usability and streamline complex workflows. My work includes designing intuitive 3D printing software, printer applications, and interactive elements that improve the overall user experience.

Beyond design, I have hands-on experience in Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), enabling me to collaborate effectively with developers and bridge the gap between design and implementation. My goal is to create user-centered solutions that not only enhance experiences but also drive business value.

Let's connect and build seamless, engaging digital experiences together!
